Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

Delicious Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

These Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars are delicious, fast and easy to make due to the "no bake" method

With this recipe you can easily play around with the ingredients based on your preference, let's go!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 ¾ cups confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together graham cracker crumbs, confectioners' sugar, peanut butter and melted butter
  2. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan.
  3. Melt chocolate chips over a double boiler or in the microwave, stirring occasionally.
  4. Spread the melted chocolate over the crumb crust.
  5. Chill for about 5 minutes.
  6. Cut into bars before the chocolate is completely set.
  7. Chill until ready to serve.

Playing around with the ingredients

With this recipe you can for example swap the chocolate for pure or white chocolate to your personal preference, the same goes for the peanut butter.
